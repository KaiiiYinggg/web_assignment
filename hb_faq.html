FAQ:

When do you deliver?

Orders will be shipped within 5 to 7 working days (excluding Saturdays, Sundays and public holidays) for Pen. Deliveries are made Mondays to Fridays (except public holidays).

How do I make payment?

We accept all major cards (Visa, Master, Amex, Credit, and Debit) as form of payments.

Can I change the type of cookies after placing your order?

Unfortunately, amendments to the type of cake are not permissible after placing your order.

When will the sold out items be available?

Our sold out items typically take 1 week or less to restock under the current circumstances.
